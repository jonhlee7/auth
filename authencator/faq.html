Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Authenticator App. If you don’t see what you’re looking for, contact us at support@yourapp.com.

What is Authenticator App? +
Authenticator App is a free, open-source two-factor authentication (2FA) app that generates time-based one-time passwords (TOTP) for your online accounts, helping you add an extra layer of security.
Is Authenticator App free to use? +
Yes, Authenticator App is completely free to download and use. There are no hidden fees or subscriptions required for core features.
Does Authenticator App store my accounts online? +
No. All your account data is stored locally on your device. We do not sync or store your 2FA codes on external servers.
How do I add a new account? +
You can add a new account by scanning a QR code or manually entering the secret key provided by your service provider. The app will automatically generate time-based codes for you.
What happens if I lose my phone? +
Since all data is stored locally, you will need to set up your accounts again on a new device using the backup codes provided by each service. We recommend keeping backup codes in a secure place.
Is my data encrypted? +
Yes. Your 2FA account data is encrypted on your device to protect it from unauthorized access.
