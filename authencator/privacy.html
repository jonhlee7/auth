Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the "Authenticator App" mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

I. Introduction

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our App. We do not collect or sell your personal data. The App is designed to store all 2FA data locally on your device.

If you are a California resident, you may request details about the information we process. We do not sell personal data.

By using the App, you acknowledge that you have read and agree to this Privacy Policy.

II. Information We Process

The App is designed to operate locally on your device and does not transmit your 2FA accounts, codes, or secrets to external servers.

We may collect limited device and usage data for analytics and crash reporting purposes only, such as device type, operating system, app version, and crash logs. This data is used to improve the App and is not linked to your personal identity.

We do not collect location data, contact data, or sensitive personal information unless you voluntarily provide it.

Payment information for any subscriptions is processed directly by Apple Inc. We do not receive or store your payment details.

III. Purpose of Data Processing

We use any collected data only to:

• Provide, maintain, and improve the App
• Fix technical issues and optimize performance
• Ensure security and prevent fraud
• Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your data for marketing without your explicit consent.

IV. Sharing Your Information

We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose your personal information to third parties for commercial purposes.

We may share limited, non-identifiable data with trusted service providers who assist in operating the App, such as:

• Apple Inc. (cloud services, payment processing)
• Firebase / Google Analytics (app analytics and crash reporting)

These providers are bound by their own privacy policies. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

V. International Data Transfer

Any non-identifiable analytics data may be transferred to other countries for processing. We take reasonable measures to protect such data.

VI. Your Privacy Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any data we hold about you. Since the App stores all core data locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by uninstalling the App.

To exercise your rights, contact us using the information below.

VII. Data Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect any information we process. All 2FA data is stored locally and encrypted on your device. We cannot guarantee absolute security over the Internet, but we take industry-standard precautions.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after updates constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
